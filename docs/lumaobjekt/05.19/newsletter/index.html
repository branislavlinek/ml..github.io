LUMA OBJEKT je na trhu viac ako 20 rokov. Úspešne sme podnikali v oblasti kancelárskeho nábytku a trezorovej techniky, kde sme zastupovali svetovú špičku – amerického výrobcu Steelcase, nemeckých výrobcov Werndl a Mauser. Pri kontakte s našimi zákazníkmi sme sa stretávali so záumom o doplnky, medzi inými aj o vonné a dekoratívne sviečky.  

  LADENAC MILANO is luxury, in a word. That luxury with character. Sober, discreet, elegant, sophisticated, a smell that is truly enjoyed, that makes you feel good with small details and without overloading the senses.
It is born from the need to narrate a voyage through a sensory journey where the aromas, fragrances and characteristic materials of the Italian lands are exalted.
– 100% vegetal wax, consisting of soya, mango and coco
– 0% parafine
– handpoured
– fragrances from essential oils and parfumes
